About


Travelling is common for all of us. However, we always don't know all the information about a place we want to go to. Sometimes we want to go somewhere, but we don’t know which transportation would be the best for us or worse we even don’t know the fare and some people take advantage of that. Sometimes, we all wish that we had some kind of information before going somewhere unknown. That’s where our product is helpful. Our final product will be a website where all this information will be available. Here users have to sign in for their account. Users will enter a starting place and a destination, and our software will lead him/her through the shortest and easiest path. A user can both give and receive information about the desired destination they are looking for. A user can see the information that already exists, or they can even ask new queries. They can rate the information whether it was helpful or not. When a user is giving information then their interface will switch to guide mode. In the guide mode, users can answer queries. For every ‘helpful’ rating, the guide earns 10 points. When a guide reaches 10,000 points, they can withdraw the points as real money; (here for 10,000 points they will get 100 takas). Users can also update their personal information in their profiles. Both user and guide can report their problems so that the admin can solve the bug or issues that they are facing.
